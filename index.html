The I²IT Alumni Association is a student-led initiative dedicated to building lasting connections between our alumni and the institute. Developed and maintained by the passionate students of the International Institute of Information Technology, this platform aims to foster a thriving community of graduates, mentors, and innovators.

Our mission is to celebrate the achievements of our alumni, encourage collaboration, and provide opportunities for networking, mentorship, and growth. Whether you're a recent graduate or a seasoned professional, this space is your bridge back to the IIIT family.

We believe in the power of shared experiences and collective progress—and we're committed to keeping the spirit of IIIT alive across generations.

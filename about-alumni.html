About Alumni Portal

Whether you've just graduated this year or you've been part of IT for years, this is the place to connect to all things and all I2IT family members. Socialize, network, share news, and get involved with fellow alumni, students, faculty, staff, and friends. Get access to the people, events, ideas, and resources that matter to you, and help strengthen the Institution. You became a part of this association the day you joined this campus and became part of the I2IT family. We are here to help you keep that bond strong. You can find friends old and new and be part of events on campus, conduct sessions, connect and communicate with ex-I2ITians or just offer your support to your alma mater. Whatever you choose, we are here to help you be in touch!
